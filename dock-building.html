Which Dock Type is Right for Your Muskoka Property?

Different shorelines and water depths call for different approaches. Here's a quick overview of the most common dock types we build.

Floating Docks

Ideal for properties with fluctuating water levels or rocky shorelines. Floating docks rise and fall with the water and are removable in the fall — a popular choice in Muskoka for seasonal cottages.

Pipe Docks

Versatile and cost-effective, pipe docks work well in moderate water depths with a stable lake bed. Easy to install and remove seasonally, making them great for cottages with shallow, sandy shores.

Permanent Crib Docks

The gold standard for deep-water Muskoka properties. Crib docks are built to last generations and are ideal for year-round properties or where heavy boat traffic demands a solid, stable platform.
