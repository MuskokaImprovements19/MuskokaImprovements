Our Most Popular Renovation Projects

Every renovation is different, but these are the projects we're most frequently asked about.

Kitchen Renovations

A new kitchen is the single biggest way to improve how your cottage or home functions and feels. We handle full kitchen renovations including cabinetry, countertops, flooring, lighting, plumbing rough-in, and everything in between.

Bathroom Renovations

From a simple refresh to a complete gut-and-tile rebuild, we renovate bathrooms of every size and style. Tile work, vanity installs, shower surrounds, and fixtures — handled with care and precision.

Full Property Renovations

Buying a dated Muskoka property and transforming it? We take on full-scope renovation projects — managing subcontractors, timelines, and every phase of work so you don't have to.

How We Approach Every Renovation

We start with a thorough site visit and honest conversation about your goals and budget. From there we put together a detailed quote with a clear scope of work and project timeline.
