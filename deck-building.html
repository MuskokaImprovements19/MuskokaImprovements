Deck Building in Muskoka

A well-built deck transforms your Muskoka property — giving you more space to enjoy the outdoors, entertain guests, and take in everything cottage country has to offer. At Muskoka Improvements, we design and build custom decks for cottages and homes throughout Muskoka.


Whether you need a pressure-treated wood deck, a premium composite deck, or a multi-level design that maximizes your lake views, our experienced team will bring your vision to life.

Serving Bracebridge, Huntsville, Gravenhurst & All of Muskoka

From Lake Muskoka to Lake Rosseau — we build decks throughout cottage country.

Our Deck Building Services Include:

  • Pressure-treated wood decks
  • Composite and PVC low-maintenance decks
  • Multi-level and wraparound deck designs
  • Waterfront and lakefront decks
  • Deck additions and extensions
  • Deck railings, stairs and lighting
  • Pergolas and covered outdoor structures
  • Deck repairs, refinishing and replacement

Why choose Muskoka Improvements for your deck? We have been building in cottage country since 2019. We know Muskoka's climate, the freeze-thaw cycles that stress outdoor structures, and the permitting requirements for waterfront builds. Every deck we build is designed to last decades.
